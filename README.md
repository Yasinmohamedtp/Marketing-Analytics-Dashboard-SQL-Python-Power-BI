# 📊 Marketing Analytics Dashboard | SQL + Python + Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Marketing Analytics Dashboard** built for **ShopEasy**, an online retail company facing declining customer engagement and conversion rates despite increased marketing investments.

The solution integrates **SQL**, **Python**, and **Power BI** to transform raw business data into meaningful insights. It combines marketing performance metrics, customer journey analysis, and sentiment analysis of customer reviews to help stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

ShopEasy has recently encountered several business challenges:

- 📉 Declining customer engagement across marketing channels.
- 🛒 Falling conversion rates despite increasing website traffic.
- 💰 Rising marketing expenses with lower-than-expected returns.
- ⭐ Customer feedback indicating mixed satisfaction levels.
- 📊 Need for a centralized analytics solution to monitor KPIs and identify improvement opportunities.

The objective of this project is to analyze customer behavior, marketing effectiveness, and review sentiment to uncover actionable business insights.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **SQL** – Data cleaning, transformation, and modeling
- **Python** – Customer review preprocessing and sentiment analysis
- **Power BI** – Interactive dashboards and KPI visualization
- **Excel/CSV** – Intermediate data storage

# 📊 Dashboard Overview

The Power BI report consists of **four interactive pages**, each designed to answer a specific business question.

## 1️⃣ Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of business performance through key KPIs and trend visualizations.

### KPIs
- Conversion Rate
- Total Views
- Total Clicks
- Total Likes
- Average Customer Rating

### Visuals
- Monthly Conversion Trend
- Conversion Rate by Product
- Views, Clicks & Likes Trend
- Monthly Rating Trend
- Product-wise Rating Comparison

---

## 2️⃣ Conversion Analysis

Analyzes customer behavior throughout the purchase journey.

### Features
- Overall Conversion Rate KPI
- Customer Journey Funnel (View → Click → Drop-off → Purchase)
- Monthly Conversion Trends
- Product-wise Conversion Performance
- Conversion Matrix by Product and Month

This page helps identify where customers leave the purchasing process and which products convert the best.

---

## 3️⃣ Social Media Engagement

Measures the effectiveness of marketing campaigns and content performance.

### Features
- Total Views, Clicks, and Likes
- Monthly Engagement Trends
- Views by Content Type (Blog, Social Media, Video)
- Product-wise Engagement Heatmap

This dashboard helps evaluate audience interaction across different marketing channels.

---

## 4️⃣ Customer Reviews & Sentiment Analysis

Combines customer ratings with Python-based sentiment analysis.

### Features
- Average Customer Rating
- Rating Distribution
- Sentiment Category Distribution
- Monthly Rating Trends by Sentiment
- Customer Review Table

This page provides insights into customer satisfaction and recurring feedback patterns.

---

# 🔍 Key Insights

### 📉 1. Conversion Rate is Low

The overall conversion rate is **9.57%**, indicating that only a small percentage of visitors ultimately complete a purchase.

---

### 🚪 2. High Customer Drop-off Before Purchase

The customer journey analysis shows significant drop-offs between product views, clicks, and completed purchases, highlighting opportunities to optimize the conversion funnel.

---

### 🏆 3. Top-Performing Products

Products such as:

- Hockey Stick
- Ski Boots
- Baseball Glove
- Cycling Helmet

demonstrate higher conversion rates and can serve as benchmarks for other product categories.

---

### 📱 4. Marketing Engagement is Declining

Views, clicks, and likes steadily decrease over time, suggesting that current marketing campaigns may require optimization or refreshed content strategies.

---

### 🎥 5. Content Performance Varies by Type

Different content formats (Blog, Social Media, and Video) contribute differently to audience engagement, enabling better allocation of marketing resources.

---

### ⭐ 6. Customer Satisfaction is Moderate

The average customer rating of **3.69/5** indicates generally positive experiences but also highlights room for improvement in product quality and customer service.

---

### 😊 7. Positive Sentiment Dominates Reviews

Sentiment analysis reveals that most customer reviews are positive, although mixed and negative sentiments indicate recurring issues that should be addressed.

---

### 📊 8. Product Performance Differs Across Metrics

Some products perform well in conversions but receive average customer ratings, while others have strong customer satisfaction but lower conversion rates. This provides opportunities for targeted business improvements.

---

# 💡 Recommendations

- Improve the checkout and purchasing experience to reduce customer drop-offs.
- Study and replicate the strategies behind top-converting products.
- Refresh marketing campaigns to reverse declining engagement trends.
- Invest more in content formats that consistently drive user interactions.
- Leverage sentiment analysis to identify and address recurring customer pain points.
- Continuously monitor KPIs using interactive dashboards to support data-driven decision-making.
